Nicole Gorcinski licensed skin-care esthetician

TRIO Salon is pleased to feature respected skin care expert Nicole Gorcinski.

Nicole has been a licensed skin-care esthetician since 1983, specializing in highly effective glycolic treatments and microdermabrasion. Nicole graduated from Christine Valmy Skincare Academy and has an extremely impressive resume. Nicole previously worked for the Bergdorf Goodman Salon and Spa in New York and Anna Kay Skin Care Salon in Chicago.

Every skin care consultation and treatment with Nicole is personalized based on your skin type and condition, age, medical history, and life-style. Nicole carefully analyzes your skin’s condition to match it with the most appropriate and advanced skin-care treatments. In addition, Nicole helps plan your home skin-care regimen so you can achieve the results you desire and deserve.

Treatments

Microdermabrasion

Helps to reduce wrinkles and fine lines, age spots, light acne scars, and undesired pigmentation that may appear on the skin. It removes the outermost layer of dry, dead skin cells to reveal a younger, healthier-looking skin. Microdermabrasion also encourages the production of a new underlying layer of skin cells with higher levels of collagen and elastin, which further improves the skin's appearance. $120 $200 with facial

Glycolic/Lactic
